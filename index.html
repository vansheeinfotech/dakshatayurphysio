What We Do

We Offer Various Ayurveda & Physio Service

We offer a unique combination of Panchakarma, traditional Ayurvedic therapies, and effective Physiotherapy treatments.

Abhyanga (Ayurvedic Full Body Massage)

A traditional warm oil massage that enhances circulation and promotes deep relaxation. It helps detoxify the body and rejuvenate the mind.

Read More
Shirodhara (Oil Dripping Therapy)

A steady stream of medicated oil is gently poured over the forehead. It calms the nervous system and is effective for stress, insomnia, and headaches.

Read More
Basti (Medicated Enema Therapy)

A cleansing treatment using herbal oils or decoctions through the rectum. It balances Vata dosha and supports digestive and joint health.

Read More
Nasya (Nasal Detoxification Therapy)

Herbal oils are administered through the nose to cleanse the sinuses. It aids in relieving allergies, migraines, and respiratory issues.

Read More
Physiotherapy (Manual Therapy)

Hands-on techniques to directly manipulate and move soft tissues and joints, aiming to improve range of motion, reduce pain, and enhance movement patterns.

Read More
Electrotherapy (IFT, TENS, Ultrasound)

Non-invasive treatments using electrical stimulation for pain relief and muscle stimulation. Ideal for sports injuries and chronic pain.

Read More
Exercise Therapy & Rehabilitation

Customized exercises to help individuals to restore strength, flexibility, and function. Tailored rehab plans for post-injury or post-surgery recovery.

Postural Correction & Ergonomic Advice

Assessment and training to improve posture and workplace ergonomics. Helps prevent musculoskeletal problems and back pain.
